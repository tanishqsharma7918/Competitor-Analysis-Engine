🚀 **Competitor Analysis Engine**

**AI-Powered Competitive Benchmarking Platform**
Designed & developed by **Tanishq Sharma**

The Competitor Analysis Engine is an intelligent, fully automated system that analyzes any product against its competitive landscape. It uses GPT-powered research, feature extraction, and strategic insight generation to deliver fast, accurate, and actionable market intelligence.

Perfect for portfolios, interviews, product strategy explorations, and real-world competitive benchmarking.

Live URL : https://competitor-analysis-engine.streamlit.app/

### ✨ Key Features

#### 🔍 **Automated Competitor Discovery**

AI instantly identifies top competitors based on your product input.

#### 🧩 **Feature Comparison Matrix**

A structured, easy-to-read comparison showing:

* Your product
* Key competitors
* Top 7 critical features mapped across all products

#### 🧠 **AI-Generated Insights**

Includes:

* Unique differentiators
* Actionable strategic recommendations
* Top 5 missing capabilities
* Market positioning insights
* Opportunities to strengthen your product

#### 📊 **Exportable Reports**

Download professional-grade outputs in multiple formats:

* **Excel (.xlsx)** — full analysis
* **PowerPoint (.pptx)** — ready-to-present slides
  

#### 🎨 **Modern Streamlit UI**

* Fast & responsive
* Clean and minimal
* No database required
* Fully stateless
